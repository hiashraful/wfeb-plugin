1. What Is This Plugin?

The WFEB plugin turns footballexams.com into a football skills certification platform. Here is what it does in simple terms:

  • Coaches sign up on the website, get approved by the admin, and then can test players on their football skills.
  • Players are added by their coach. They get tested on 7 different football skills (passing, shooting, sprinting, etc.).
  • After a test, the player receives a certificate with their score and achievement level (Bronze, Silver, Gold, etc.).
  • Coaches need to buy credits to issue certificates. 1 credit = 1 certificate.
  • Anyone in the world can verify a certificate is real by checking it on the website.

There are three separate dashboards:

  1. Admin Dashboard — The admin manages everything from here.
  2. Coach Dashboard — Where coaches manage their players and conduct exams.
  3. Player Dashboard — Where players view their certificates and scores.

4. How a Coach Registers Implemented

When a new coach wants to join the platform, here is what happens step by step:

1 The coach visits the registration page:

https://footballexams.com/coach-registration/

2 They fill in the form with:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Address
  • NGB Number (coaching licence number from their national governing body)
  • Coaching Certificate (upload a photo or PDF of the coaching qualification)
  • Password (at least 8 characters)
  • Tick the box to agree to the terms

3 They click "Register". The system creates their account but marks it as "Pending". They cannot log in yet.

4 The admin receives a notification. The admin goes to the admin panel and approves or rejects the coach (see next section).

5 Once approved, the coach receives an email saying they can now log in.

Important: Coaches cannot do anything until the admin approves them. This protects the platform from unqualified people.

5. Approving or Rejecting a Coach Implemented

When a new coach registers, the admin needs to review and approve them. Here is how:

1 Go to the admin area and click WFEB → Coaches:

https://footballexams.com/wp-admin/admin.php?page=wfeb-coaches

2 Look for coaches with status "Pending" (shown in yellow/orange).

3 Click on the coach's name to open their profile.

4 Review their details:

  • Check name and contact details
  • Look at the NGB number (can be verified externally)
  • View the uploaded coaching certificate document

5 Make a decision:

  • To approve: Click the "Approve" button. The coach receives an email and can now log in.
  • To reject: Type in a reason (e.g. "Certificate expired") and click "Reject". The coach receives an email explaining why.
Tip: An approved coach can also be suspended later if needed. Go to their profile and change the status.

7. How a Coach Adds a Player Implemented

Players do not register themselves. A coach creates their account. Here is how:

1 The coach goes to My Players in the dashboard:

https://footballexams.com/coach-dashboard/?section=my-players

2 They click the "Add New Player" button.

3 A form pops up asking for:

  • Player's full name
  • Player's date of birth
  • Player's email address

4 They click "Save".

5 The system automatically:

  • Creates a login account for the player
  • Generates a temporary password
  • Sends the player a welcome email with login details

The player can now log in at https://footballexams.com/player-login/ using the email and temporary password from the email.

Tip: Coaches should make sure they use the player's correct email address, because the login details are sent there.

8. How a Coach Conducts an Exam Implemented

Once a coach has added players and has credits, they can conduct an exam:

1 The coach goes to Conduct Exam in the dashboard:

https://footballexams.com/coach-dashboard/?section=conduct-exam

2 Select the player from a dropdown list.

3 Enter the exam date.

4 Optionally add an assistant examiner name.

5 Fill in the scores for all 7 categories (see Section 9 for details):

  1. Short Passing — Left foot and right foot (0-5 each)
  2. Long Passing — Left foot and right foot (0-5 each)
  3. Shooting — Four corners of the goal (0-5 each)
  4. Sprint — Time in seconds (converted to a score automatically)
  5. Dribble — Time in seconds (converted to a score automatically)
  6. Kickups — Three attempts, best count is used (converted to a score automatically)
  7. Volley — Left foot and right foot (0-5 each)

6 The coach can either:

  • Save as Draft — Come back and finish it later. No credit is used.
  • Complete Exam — Finalise the exam. This uses 1 credit and generates a certificate.
Important: Once an exam is completed, it cannot be changed. All scores must be correct before clicking "Complete".

7 After completing, the system automatically:

  • Calculates the total score (out of 80)
  • Assigns the achievement level (e.g. Gold, Silver)
  • Generates a certificate with a unique number (e.g. WFEB-1042)
  • Creates a downloadable PDF certificate
  • Sends the player an email with their results
  • Deducts 1 credit from the coach's balance

10. Certificates & Credits

How Certificates Work Implemented

  • Every completed exam generates a unique certificate.
  • Certificates have a number like WFEB-1000, WFEB-1001, etc.
  • A PDF version is automatically created and can be downloaded by the coach or the player.
  • Anyone can verify a certificate at https://footballexams.com/verify-certificate/.

How Credits Work Not Implemented

The credit purchase system will be implemented using a custom checkout powered by WooCommerce and Stripe. Coaches will purchase credits at a set price per credit. 1 credit = 1 certificate.

Revoking a Certificate Implemented

If a certificate needs to be invalidated (e.g. fraud or a mistake):

  1. Go to WFEB → Certificates in the admin panel
  2. Find the certificate and click on it
  3. Click "Revoke" and type in the reason
  4. The certificate will now show as "Revoked" and will fail verification on the public page

11. How Coaches Buy Credits Not Implemented

  • Custom checkout page within the coach dashboard

  • Stripe for secure card payments

Once live, coaches will be able to buy credits directly from their dashboard, and credits will be added to their account automatically after payment.

13. How Anyone Can Verify a Certificate Implemented

This is one of the most important features. Anyone (a football club, an employer, a parent) can check if a certificate is genuine.

1 Visit the verification page:

https://footballexams.com/verify-certificate/

2 Enter three pieces of information:

  • Player's Name (does not need to be exact — a partial match works)
  • Certificate Number (e.g. WFEB-1042)
  • Player's Date of Birth

3 Click "Verify".

4 If the certificate is real, the following details are shown:

  • Player's name
  • Total score
  • Achievement level
  • Exam date
  • Certificate number
  • Examiner's name

If the certificate does not exist, or the details do not match, an error message is shown.

Note: If a certificate has been revoked by the admin, it will show as invalid on this page.

16. Common Questions

A coach says they cannot log in. What should the admin do?

Check two things:

  1. Go to WFEB → Coaches and check if the coach's status is "Pending". If so, they need to be approved first.
  2. If the coach is already approved, suggest they reset their password at https://footballexams.com/forgot-password/.

A coach says they cannot complete an exam. Why?

They likely have 0 credits. Check their credit balance from WFEB → Coaches by clicking on their name. They will need more credits to continue.

Can a player register on their own?

No. Players must be added by their coach. The player receives a welcome email with login details automatically.

Can the admin give a coach free credits?

Yes. Go to WFEB → Coaches, click on the coach's name, and adjust their credit balance.

A certificate was issued by mistake. Can it be cancelled?

Yes. Go to WFEB → Certificates, find the certificate, and click "Revoke". A reason must be typed in. The certificate will no longer pass verification.

Can coaches see each other's players?

No. Each coach can only see their own players. Only the admin can see all players and all coaches.

Is there a dark mode?

Yes. Both the coach and player dashboards have a theme toggle (sun/moon icon in the top-right corner) to switch between light and dark mode. The default is light mode.

Something is broken or not working. Who should be contacted?

Contact the developer (Devash) at devash.pro.
